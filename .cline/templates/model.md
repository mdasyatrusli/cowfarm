# Model Template

## Purpose

Template standar Model Laravel.

---

## Naming

Singular PascalCase

Contoh:

Cow

Farm

MilkRecord

---

## Required

- Fillable
- Relationship
- Scope (jika perlu)

---

## Relationship

hasMany

belongsTo

belongsToMany

---

## Rules

Model tidak boleh berisi:

- business logic besar
- proses validasi

---

## Checklist

☐ Fillable

☐ Relationship

☐ Scope

☐ SoftDeletes (jika diperlukan)
